Our cuisine

We choose only local ingredients and fish from the Apulian coast to offer an authentic, flavor-rich experience.

The flavor of our land

Bari cuisine is made of ancient gestures: handmade pasta, the scent of the sea early in the morning, and Sundays spent with family. At La Locanda dell'Elfo we wanted to preserve all of this, bringing the true essence of Puglia to the plate. Because eating with us means truly experiencing Bari, not just visiting it.

Bari Vecchia: our heart

La Locanda dell'Elfo is set among the evocative alleys of Bari Vecchia, just steps from the sea and the city's thousand-year history.

Local cooking

Honest recipes, calmer composition, and a richer Mediterranean mood.

Tradition Philosophy

A trattoria that speaks Bari even when it stays elegant.

A journey through tradition and the sea.

Our menu changes throughout the year to respect seasonality and the freshness of ingredients, but the promise always stays the same: genuine dishes, intense flavors, and portions that smell like home.

From handmade pasta to seafood crudi, all the way to grilled fish main courses, every proposal is created to celebrate the richness of Apulian cuisine.

We choose only local ingredients and fish from the Apulian coast to offer an authentic and flavor-rich experience.

Our philosophy? Respect for tradition, uncompromising quality, and sincere hospitality.
